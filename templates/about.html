About LinemanStormCalls

Built by linemen to simplify storm call visibility.

Why this exists

Storm work opportunities are often scattered across individual contractor rosters, word-of-mouth, and social media groups. LinemanStormCalls is a simple, centralized notice board that helps linemen see storm mobilization notices in one place.

What we do

  • Provide visibility to storm mobilization notices.
  • Keep the platform public and easy to browse for linemen.
  • Allow approved contractors to post their own onboarding links or instructions.

What we do not do

  • We do not dispatch workers.
  • We do not hire on behalf of contractors.
  • We do not manage claims, assignments, or roster placement.
Important: All work must clear through the appropriate IBEW locals and the contractor’s own onboarding process. LinemanStormCalls is informational only.
